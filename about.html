Why We Started

Have you ever felt lost in a museum? Surrounded by countless treasures, but not sure which ones truly matter… You glance at the “must-see” exhibits, take a quick photo, and move on—only to forget what you just saw. Listening to long official guides often feels heavy and tiring.

Our Idea

We created the Museum Deck series to change that experience. By combining education, technology, art, and playful design, we want to make museum visits more fun, engaging, and memorable. Imagine turning your visit into a treasure hunt—exploring, discovering, and learning in a way that feels natural and enjoyable.

First Stop: The MET

Our first product is dedicated to the Metropolitan Museum of Art (The MET). With its vast collection and iconic masterpieces, The MET can be overwhelming. Our guide highlights what to see, why it matters, and helps you carry the stories with you long after you leave the museum.

What Makes It Different

• Not just facts, but stories that stick.
• Not just listening, but playing and exploring.
• Not just a visit, but an experience that inspires curiosity.
